âœ¨ Features / Highlights
The primary goal of this dashboard is to provide clear, actionable insights into the Netflix content strategy.

Business Problem: The sheer volume and diversity of content on Netflix make it challenging to quickly grasp the nature of the library, the pace of content acquisition, and key focus areas for production and licensing.

Goal of the Dashboard: To create an intuitive, visual interface that allows users to segment the library and easily track content diversity, geographical focus, and changing consumption trends.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals (Hypothetical)
Content Volume KPIs: Highlighting the Total Count of Movies and Total Count of TV Shows in the library.

Geographic Content Focus: A map or ranked list showing the countries with the highest volume of content production/release.

Genre and Top Creator Analysis: Visualizations ranking the most popular Genres and listing Top Directors or Actors/Actresses based on the number of titles they are associated with.
